Campaign Overview

Hocco, a leading ice cream brand in Gujarat, launched the ‘Hooked on Hocco’ summer campaign to strengthen its market presence post-rebranding in 2022. Leveraging its prominent retail presence, the campaign used vibrant out-of-home (OOH) advertisements and whimsical mascot-driven storytelling to stand out in a competitive market. The goal was to boost brand visibility, increase mascot popularity, and evoke the joy of summer through Hocco’s diverse flavors and formats, as celebrated by Hocco founder Ankit Chona: “The campaign hit it out of the park!”

Campaign Strategy

The ‘Hooked on Hocco’ remorse reimagined summer in the Hoccoverse, where mascots embodied the ice cream obsession sparked by rising temperatures. It focused on:

  • Visual Differentiation: Vibrant, mascot-led visuals set Hocco apart from competitors like Amul and Vadilal in Gujarat’s high-sales ice cream market.
  • Emotional Connection: Whimsical scenarios captured the joy of cooling off with Hocco’s flavors (e.g., kulfi, Boss Bars), evoking nostalgia and indulgence.
  • Multi-Format Engagement: Promoted diverse products (cups, cones, tubs, novelty items) across company-owned outlets, general trade, and quick-commerce platforms like Blinkit.
  • OOH Impact: Eye-catching billboards and animations amplified brand presence in urban and semi-urban areas, driving 15–20% sales growth during summer.

The campaign’s tagline, “Hooked on Hocco,” celebrated endless flavor exploration, aligning with Hocco’s promise of “old taste, new vibe.”
