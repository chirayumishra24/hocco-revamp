Hocco’s Game Plan: Winning Hearts

Storytelling

“House of Chona Collaboratives” links to Chona Family’s legacy, signaling honesty and heritage.

Trusted Taste

Retained Ice Cream’s creamy quality with bold flavors like black sesame.

Modern Appeal

Vibrant packaging and quick commerce (Zepto, Blinkit) made Hocco trendy.

This blend of nostalgia and innovation built trust through word-of-mouth and reviews.

Trust: Secured, Taste: Delivered

Building trust takes consistency and engagement. By FY25, Hocco’s 15,000 retail touchpoints and 150 parlors showed growing acceptance. Quick commerce (7–10% of revenue) helped test markets efficiently.
