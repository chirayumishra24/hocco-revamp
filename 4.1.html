Benefits of the BMC

The BMC provides a clear framework for Hocco’s strategy:

Holistic View

Maps components like customer segments and revenue streams for strategic clarity.

Customer Focus

Targets Gen Z, families, and quick commerce users, driving 20% quick commerce revenue.

Revenue Diversification

Combines franchise fees, catering, and premium sales for Rs 420–450 crore by FY26.

Efficiency

Optimizes factory output (40,000 liters/day) and logistics.

Scalability

Supports expansion via 10,000+ retail stores and 80 SMB partnerships.

Key Metrics

Evaluate Hocco’s BMC with these metrics:

Customer Segments

Cover 3–5 segments (e.g., Gen Z, families) with 80–100% alignment.

Revenue Growth

Achieve 20–30% annual increase (Rs 220 crore FY25 to Rs 420–450 crore FY26).

Channel Reach

Expand to 10,000+ retail stores, 100+ parlors, 20% quick commerce revenue.

Cost Efficiency

Keep production/logistics at 50–60% of expenses.

Key Engagement

Reach 70–80% via loyalty programs and social media.

Brand Consistency

Ensure 100% alignment with Scooperheroes and preservative-free identity.
