🍦Assignment: 3D Design Hocco Land Innovation Lab

Hocco’s Virtual Internship – Experiential Activity

Hocco Internship

Assignment Overview

In this experiential activity, you’ll design a Hocco Land Theme Park using the embedded Hocco Land Innovation Lab 3D tool below. Your task is to create a unique theme park concept inspired by Hocco’s ice cream legacy, download a picture of your design, and export metrics to showcase its appeal. You’ll also propose a branding idea and a community engagement plan to build excitement and trust. This assignment brings Hocco’s bold and nostalgic brand to life in a fun, immersive way.

Format: 1–2 page document, slideshow, or short video pitch.

Deadline: End of Module 2.

Problem Statement

Problem Statement: Your challenge is to design a Hocco Land Theme Park that captures the essence of Hocco’s ice cream legacy, integrating 8–12 attractions and 3–5 themed zones while achieving a visitor capacity of 5,000–10,000 daily.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Interact with the Hocco Land Ice Cream Innovation Lab

Use the embedded Hocco Land Ice Cream Innovation Lab below to design your Hocco Land Theme Park. Explore the interface to create attractions, themed areas, and interactive elements inspired by Hocco’s ice cream legacy.

2

Design Hocco Land with Key Metrics

Incorporate the key metrics listed below. For example, design 8–12 attractions like a Sitaphal Splash Zone and set a visitor capacity of 5,000–10,000. Use Hocco’s colors (navy, red) and flavors (e.g., Rajbhog, Shahi Pistachio) to theme your park.

3

Download Picture and Metrics

Use the Flavor Forge tool to download a picture of your Hocco Land design and export metrics (e.g., visitor capacity, revenue projections). Ensure the metrics include visitor satisfaction and branding integration percentages for your submission.

4

Develop a Branding Idea

Propose a branding concept for Hocco Land’s launch. Examples include:

  • Visual Identity: Use Hocco’s red and navy palette for park signage and uniforms, with a tagline like “Scoop the Adventure!”
  • Storytelling: Create a narrative around the Chona family’s ice cream legacy, with a “Hocco Heritage Trail” attraction.
  • Marketing Launch: Host a grand opening with free ice cream samples and a social media contest using #HoccoLandVibes.
5

Create a Community Plan

Outline how Hocco Land can build trust and excitement with visitors:

  • Local Engagement: Partner with schools for discounted group visits to Hocco Land’s “Flavor Fiesta Plaza.”
  • Interactive Events: Offer ice cream-making workshops led by Hocco chefs to engage families.
  • Transparency: Share videos of Hocco Land’s eco-friendly design (e.g., solar-powered rides) to build community trust.
6

Compile Your Submission

Combine your downloaded picture, exported metrics, branding idea, and community plan into a 1–2 page document, slideshow, or short video pitch. Ensure it reflects Hocco’s bold and nostalgic brand identity, using visuals like the scoop-bounce animation or Hocco’s color scheme. Select your file, and after selecting the file, click on Submit.

  • Strategic Decision-Making: Provides downloadable metrics to justify design choices, aiding in pitching to stakeholders.

Key Metrics for the Hocco Land Theme Park

Focus on the following metrics when designing Hocco Land Theme Park with the Hocco Land Ice Cream Innovation Lab:

  • Visitor Capacity: Aim for 5,000–10,000 daily visitors to balance scale and guest experience, inspired by medium-sized theme parks.
  • Attraction Count: Include 8–12 attractions, such as a Rajbhog Roller Coaster or Shahi Pistachio Water Slide, to reflect Hocco’s flavors.
  • Themed Areas: Design 3–5 zones (e.g., “Chona Legacy Land,” “Flavor Fiesta Plaza”) to enhance immersion and storytelling.
  • Revenue Streams: Plan for ticket sales ($20–$50 per person), food stalls ($5,000–$10,000 daily revenue), and merchandise ($2,000–$5,000 daily).
  • Engagement Metrics: Target 70–80% visitor satisfaction for attractions and 50–60% repeat visit intent based on interactive elements like ice cream workshops.
  • Branding Integration: Ensure 80–100% of attractions use Hocco’s colors (navy, red) and flavor-inspired designs for brand consistency.

Download these metrics from the Flavor Forge tool to include in your submission, showcasing Hocco Land’s potential success.
