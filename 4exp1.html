🍦Ice Cream Empire: Master the Supply Chain from Farm to Cone

Hocco’s Virtual Internship – Experiential Activity

Hocco Internship

Activity Overview

In this interactive activity, you’ll master the supply chain for Hocco’s ice cream empire. From sourcing raw materials to delivering the final product, make decisions that optimize efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure customer satisfaction. This hands-on experience demonstrates how strategic supply chain management drives success in launching and running a premium ice cream outlet.

Format: Interactive simulation with decision-making stages.

Goal: Maximize efficiency and customer happiness.

Deadline: End of Module 5.

How to Play

Ready to Start? Click on "Sourcing Raw Materials" to begin your supply chain journey! Make smart decisions at each stage to maximize efficiency and customer happiness.

1

Launch the Simulation

Use the embedded Supply Chain Simulator below to start your journey. Explore the interface and begin with sourcing decisions.

2

Make Decisions

Progress through each stage: sourcing, production, logistics, and delivery. Choose options that balance cost, quality, and speed for Hocco’s premium flavors.

3

Analyze Outcomes

Review your results at the end to see efficiency scores, costs, and customer feedback. Retry to improve your strategy.

4

Reflect and Submit

Document your best run, key decisions, and insights in a short report or slideshow. Submit at the end of this chapter.
