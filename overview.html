Welcome to the Internship

Hello, future dessert innovators! We’re excited to welcome you to the Skillizee X Hocco Virtual Internship, an immersive journey into brand building with HOCCO, India’s iconic ice cream brand redefining indulgence with a legacy of trust and innovation.

What You Will Learn

Dive deep into the art of building a beloved dessert brand with hands-on insights:

  • Brand Legacy: Understand the Chona family’s legacy and HOCCO’s birth.
  • Brand Identity: Analyze how HOCCO’s mission, vision, and unique identity connect with its heritage.
  • Product Innovation: Explore bold flavors like Rajbhog and Shahi Pistachio and their role in creating memorable experiences.
  • Go-To-Market Strategies: Learn to craft marketing and distribution strategies blending nostalgia and modernity.
  • Business Operations: Gain insights into supply chain, innovation, and financial strategies driving HOCCO’s growth.
  • Hands-On Experience: Create branding, marketing, and strategic materials reflecting HOCCO’s mission.

Quizzes and Assignments

As you progress, demonstrate your mastery:

  • Quizzes: Achieve a passing grade of 75% to unlock the next module.
  • Assignments: Earn a passing grade of 60% to showcase your creativity. Upload and Submit Assignments
  • Capstone Project: Complete the 2 Final Capstone Project and apply all learned concepts.
