Hocco Internship

Assignment Overview

In this activity, you’ll use the embedded Ice Cream Concept Generator tool to develop customer personas for Hocco’s ice cream brand. The tool allows you to describe target customers and generate detailed personas with demographics, preferences, and behaviors. Your task is to create 3–5 personas, export them as a report, and propose a marketing strategy to engage these customers, aligning with Hocco’s nostalgic and bold brand identity. Submit your personas and strategy in a 1–2 page document, slideshow, or short video pitch.

Format: 1–2 page document, slideshow, or short video pitch.

Deadline: End of Module 4.

Benefits of Ice Cream Concept Generator

Using the Ice Cream Concept Generator to develop Hocco’s customer personas offers key advantages:

  • Rapid Persona Creation: Generates detailed personas in seconds using AI-driven prompts, saving time compared to manual research.
  • Targeted Insights: Provides demographics, preferences, and behaviors tailored to Hocco’s audience, enhancing marketing precision.
  • Brand Alignment: Ensures personas reflect Hocco’s nostalgic and bold identity (e.g., Rajbhog-loving families, adventurous Gen Z), strengthening campaign relevance.
  • Exportable Reports: Allows downloading personas as structured reports for easy integration into marketing plans, similar to export features.
  • Iterative Refinement: Enables quick adjustments via chat prompts to refine personas, ensuring alignment with Hocco’s strategic goals.
