Hocco Internship

Launching Bijlee: India’s First Caffeinated Ice Candy

Hocco’s innovative launch of Bijlee, India’s first caffeinated ice candy, exemplifies its commitment to creative marketing and consumer engagement. Collaborating with Zepto and Stride Run Club Bengaluru, Hocco kept the energy high, as shared in their LinkedIn post with 32,092 followers:

“We will not disclose how many Bijlees were consumed by the team while launching #Bijlee. But we will tell you how we launched India’s first caffeinated ice candy — from insight to ice pop ⚡”

Building Connections: Staff, Distributors, Customers

Hocco fosters strong relationships across its value chain, emphasizing trust, transparency, and shared values rooted in “Acchai & Sacchai” to build a cohesive, motivated team.

Staff Engagement

Hocco fosters trust and transparency with staff through open communication and training, rooted in “Acchai & Sacchai” values.

Distributor Partnerships

Strategic alignment with distributors ensures quality delivery and brand representation.

Customer Loyalty

Consistent quality and community-focused events build loyal brand advocates.

Cohesive Team

Shared values create a motivated workforce across the value chain.

Happiness & Wellbeing Initiatives

Hocco promotes a culture of joy and wellbeing, both internally and externally, through employee-focused initiatives and community engagement.

  • Employee Wellbeing: Health and safety standards, training programs, and supportive work environments.
  • Marketing Campaigns: Emphasize joy, togetherness, and “chilling out,” resonating with consumers.
  • Community Engagement: Participation in food drives and social campaigns to foster positivity.

Hocco Ice Cream x Zepto x Stride Run Club Bengaluru collaboration to keep the Josh High.

Inclusive and Accessible Brand Building

Hocco strives to be an “arms-wide-open” brand, appealing to diverse demographics through affordability and cultural resonance.

  • Accessibility: Affordable pricing ensures products reach families and youth without compromising quality.
  • Cultural Relevance: Limited editions like Modak ice cream for Ganesh Chaturthi celebrate regional tastes.
  • Inclusive Spaces: Digital platforms and parlors create welcoming environments for all.

Conclusion

Hocco’s marketing revolution, exemplified by the Bijlee campaign, showcases its ability to blend innovation with emotional resonance. By fostering strong connections with staff, distributors, and customers. Its focus on happiness and wellbeing, through employee support and joyful campaigns, strengthens internal culture and external appeal. By prioritizing inclusivity and accessibility, Hocco ensures its brand resonates with diverse audiences, from festival-inspired flavors to affordable pricing. This holistic approach not only drives business growth but also positions Hocco as a beloved, socially responsible brand in the competitive ice cream industry.
