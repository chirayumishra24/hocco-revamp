The Hocco Story

Launched in October 2019, Hocco Ice Cream is a modern brand rooted in a passion for quality and innovation. With a focus on creating delightful desserts that resonate with the Indian palate, Hocco blends tradition with bold experimentation to craft memorable ice cream experiences.

Our Vision

  • To lead as a creative brand in the ice cream and food industry, known for daring experimentation and appealing to aspirational, modern consumers.
  • To expand strategically, aiming for pan-India and international presence while evolving with market trends and customer expectations.
  • To create spaces where “great ice cream moments happen when people come together,” making everyone feel they belong and are valued.

Our Mission

  • To spread happiness through products, service, and brand impact—offering authentic flavors and a joyful customer experience.
  • To uphold “Acchai & Sacchai”—values of goodness and honesty—in every scoop, serving only what the brand would confidently share with their own families.
  • To champion sustainable practices and responsible sourcing, positively impacting both community and environment.
  • To continuously innovate in flavors, presentation, and customer engagement, with an agile approach tailored to diverse regional and global tastes.

About Our Founder

Ankit Chona, A third-generation food entrepreneur with a deep passion for creating culinary experiences, our founder graduated in Business Administration and Management from Purdue University, USA, and has been immersed in the food business for over 16 years.

As the Founder and Managing Director, he leads five innovative brand concepts that have been nurtured in Gujarat and designed for global appeal. HOCCO Ice-Creams builds on his family’s legacy in the ice-cream industry, focusing on quality and innovation. HOCCO Eatery and 1944 The Hocco Kitchen are restaurant chains catering to modern Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) and Fine Dining spaces, respectively.

Throughout his career, he has been honored with numerous awards, including 'Restaurateur of the Year (India)' 2013-2014 by FHRAI, 'Restaurateur of the Year' 2015 by Times Food, and the youngest recipient of the 'Marketing Man of the Year' by Ahmedabad Management Association (AMA) in 2012. He is also a member of the Hotel and Restaurant Association, Young President’s Organization, and Entrepreneur’s Organization.

Hocco’s Journey Timeline

Click each event to uncover Hocco’s milestones!

 

2019: Hocco Launched

Hocco Ice Cream launched with a focus on quality and innovation.

 

2023: Expansion

Hocco expanded to over 60 stores across India.

 

2024: Revenue Milestone

Hocco achieved ₹220 crore in revenue.

 

2024: ₹600 Crore Valuation

In June 2024, Hocco secured ₹100 crore in funding, reaching a valuation of ₹600 crore with backing from the Chona Family Office and Sauce VC.
Series A (2024): Raised $12 million at a ₹600 crore ($70 million) valuation.

 

2025: Funding Raised

Hocco raised $10 million to fuel further growth.

 

2025: ₹1,440 Crore Valuation

As of May 26, 2025, Hocco Ice Cream’s post-money valuation reached ₹1,440 crore following the first close of its Series B funding round.
Series B (May 2025): Raised $10.3 million to expand manufacturing, distribution, new markets, and marketing efforts.
Total Raised: $30 million across all rounds.
