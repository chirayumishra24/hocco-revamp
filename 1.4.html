A Heartfelt Reunion

When the Chona family launched HOCCO, it wasn’t just a new brand — it was a homecoming. They focused on:

“We haven’t changed. We’ve just come home again.”

Building Loyalty

HOCCO fostered loyalty through:

Quick Commerce

7–10% of revenue, expected to hit 20% by FY26, making HOCCO accessible anytime.

Premium Parlors

150 outlets, growing to 250 by 2025, offering Insta-worthy experiences.

Social Engagement

Campaigns like “spot our billboards” on Instagram boosted Gen Z loyalty.

By FY25, HOCCO’s ₹220 crore revenue and ₹1,100 crore valuation showed customers believed in the brand again.

Lesson to Learn

HOCCO teaches that loyalty is built by honoring the past while embracing the present. When values stay consistent, customers follow — even under a new name. Emotional branding turns customers into advocates.

“When you value quality and focus on consumer, people are ready to follow you — even if a company is new.”

What is Brand Identity?

Imagine a brand as a person. Its name, style, voice, and values make up its identity. For HOCCO:

Name

“House of Chona” reflects family legacy.

Design

Clean, modern, yet rooted in tradition.

Promise

Old taste, new vibe.

Feel

Familiar, premium, warm.

Customers don’t just buy products — they buy trust, stories, and values. HOCCO’s identity makes customers feel: “Yes, this feels right.”

Why Brand Identity Matters

A strong brand identity is the soul of the business. HOCCO shows that staying true to roots while evolving builds trust and loyalty. Customers connect not just to the product, but to the story behind it.

By FY25, HOCCO’s ₹1,100 crore valuation proves that a clear, consistent, and meaningful identity turns good brands into unforgettable ones.

Learning Insights

Engaging Gen Z

Interactive social media campaigns, like HOCCO’s billboard contests, boost Gen Z loyalty.

Quick Commerce

Fast delivery via quick commerce builds trust and accessibility in new markets.

Memorable Identity

A clear name, design, and promise create a recognizable and trusted brand identity.

Nostalgia

Leveraging emotional memories helps reconnect customers with a rebranded company.
