Packaging That Tells a Story

Discover the magic of Hocco’s packaging – bold, colorful, and eco-friendly! It’s not just a container; it’s a canvas that sparks joy and shares our story. Unlike ordinary cartons, our Instagrammable designs turn every tub into a social media star, perfect for sharing sweet moments.

Hocco’s Style: Playful and story-driven, captivating young hearts and minds.

Inside Hocco’s Production Wonderland

Step into our enchanting Bavla plant in Gujarat, where we whip up 1.3 lakh liters of joy daily – aiming for 2.5 lakh by February 2026! Supplying over 15,000 stores and 100+ parlors in India and the USA, our cutting-edge tech and spotless hygiene ensure every scoop is fresh, preservative-free, and bursting with flavor.

Sweet Fun Fact: Our magical machines craft ice cream so pure, it’s like a hug from grandma – but colder and creamier!
