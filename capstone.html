Project Overview

Welcome to the final capstone project of the SkilliZee EcoPreneurship Internship with Hocco Ice Cream! Themed Beyond the Scoop, this project challenges you to step into the shoes of a product innovator.

Your task is to conceptualize a brand-new, unique ice cream product and pitch it to the Hocco team through a 2–3 minute video. The best pitches will be recognized by Hocco and SkilliZee, supporting Hocco’s mission of “Acchai & Sacchai” (Goodness & Truth). This task is mandatory to earn your Internship Certificate and Badge.

Project Task: Record a Product Pitch Video

Design an innovative Hocco Ice Cream product and create a 2–3 minute video pitching your concept to the executive team. Upload the video directly to the LMS.

Be Genuine

Share your passion for your new product idea and explain how it perfectly aligns with Hocco's core values.

Introduce the Product

Give your new ice cream a catchy name and clearly explain the concept (e.g., flavor profile or unique format).

Highlight the Appeal

Mention what makes it stand out—healthy alternative, nostalgic local flavor, or eco-friendly packaging?

Target Audience

Define exactly who will love this product and why it fills an exciting gap in the current market.

Descriptive Language

Use vivid, sensory words so the judges can practically taste your "refreshingly natural" creation.

Keep it Concise

Aim for 2–3 minutes of clear, focused, and compelling content.

Speak Clearly

Maintain eye contact, speak confidently, and smile naturally.

Good Production

Ensure your face is well-lit and record in a quiet environment so your pitch is easy to hear.

Call to Action

Conclude with a strong, convincing statement on why Hocco must add this to their lineup.

Submission & Rewards

Submit your capstone project by uploading your video file directly to the SkilliZee LMS in the submission portal. Top submissions earn exclusive recognition and rewards.
